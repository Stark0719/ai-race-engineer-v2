FIA Sporting Regulations (Excerpt):

Article 24:
Drivers must use at least two different dry-weather tyre compounds during a race unless it is declared wet.

Pit Lane Speed Limit:
Pit lane speed limits are enforced and time loss varies per circuit.

Safety Car Procedure:
When a safety car is deployed, the field is neutralized, and pit stop time loss is reduced due to slower overall race pace.

Virtual Safety Car:
VSC requires drivers to follow delta times, reducing advantage gained from aggressive driving.
